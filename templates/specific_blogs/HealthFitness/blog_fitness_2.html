Brain Food

Best food for brain diet.

in this article, you will learn how you should eat to maximize your brainpower increase your productivity and get healthier.

Food you should avoid

You should avoid food that contains simple sugars like chocolate, candies,
Also, I don’t recommend eating white bread.
Fast food is also bad for your brain because of the high trans fats
vodka and other alcohol of this type can harm your brain in a long run but red wine in small doses can even increase brain power
Sugary drinks don’t drink anything that contains sugar.

What food you should eat.

Morning
in the morning you should eat something that will not make you tired. These food are oatmeal, spinach, eggs, beans, and blueberries. If you combine these things together you will be feeling amazing...
Evenning
in the evening you should avoid food at all because your body will be digesting during the night. You should not eat at all because the body will not focus only on processing information but also on digesting the food we ate at night and that’s why we shouldn't eat 1-2 hours before going to bed.
Lunch
Ideal lunch is one that does not contain many carbohydrates because carbohydrates are not so beneficial for the brain, so you need to eat meat such as chicken or beef because meat contains substances that will not make you sleepy but will make you think clearly and sharper you focus.

if you start feeling hungry you can eat food that will not contain that many calories It can be, for example, tuna or sardines or wholegrain bread with cheese.

My favorite food combinations for brain health

Tuna + Spinach + Wholegrain bread + spread cheese.

Chicken + cabbage + rice

Mozzarella + olive oil + wholegrain bread with ketchup

Oatmeal + blueberries + greek yogurt

Spinach + tomatoes + blueberries + 30 g of sugar

Best supplements for software engineers

Vitamin supplementation for programmers is, in my opinion, as important as for those who go to the gym, because when a person has enough vitamins, he can get rid of brain fog and delay mental fatigue. These vitamins are omega 3, zinc, vitamin C, vitamin D, and vitamin B

I would like to say that I am not a certified nutritionist but I’ve tried a lot of diets in my life during my fitness and programming journey and I think that this is the best overall that I've tried over the past 4 years of my life.

After some time your body will start burning more fat so you will become leaner without any additional effort but everything takes time and consistency so trust the process.
