Brain Health

Why we must take care of our brain?

After I started learning how to code I started feeling a little cloudy in my head I was furious about that because I had no idea what it can cause because I was not coding that much at that time. After some time I discovered that my brain was tired, so I learned some techniques how to avoid it and how to stay laser-focused as long as possible.

1. Eat healthy food

Best food for your brain are blueberries, oats, fish, red meat, a leafy green vegetable like spinach, cabbage, etc.. , and nuts more specifically walnuts.
After I started implementing this food into my diet after 2-3 days I started seeing some results I got less tired I started sleeping better and also my focus improved and I’ve started coding more.

2. Better sleep

Don’t eat 1-2 hours before bed.
Drink some tea that will help you fall asleep before you go to bed
Don’t be on phone (read or listen to film/podcast or music do something where you don't need to consume blue light.)
Sleep for 7-8 hours (If you can sleep like this every single night you don’t need to rest on weekends you can do something productive instead)
Do breathing exercises (If you can force yourself to do 20 deep breaths your heart rate will slow down and you will get tired in an instant)
Fresh air

3. Do some exercise

The best way for me to get rid of brain fog is to take a little walk, do 1-2 minutes of cardio, or do some type of exercise like cycling, exercising, etc..

4. Drink water

Drink every hour at least 2dl of water get yourself a bottle where you will divide a bottle into lines every line will represent the amount of water that you want to drink during the time period. Water will also prevent your eyes from getting dry.

5. Avoid caffeine in the evening

when you want to work in the evening you should not drink a coffee because caffeine can stay in your blood for 8 or more hours and that will damage your sleep. If you want to learn more about the benefits that sleep gives you check the book why we sleep.

Vitamin supplementation for programmers is, in my opinion, as important as for those who go to the gym, because when a person has enough vitamins, he can get rid of brain fog and delay mental fatigue. These vitamins are omega 3, zinc, vitamin C, vitamin D, and vitamin B.
