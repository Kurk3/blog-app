My workout routine

I have already tried many types of training routines in my fitness journey and therefore I would like to summarize which is best for me and in which I have made the most progress.

Full body workout routine

I train 3-5 times a week the full body which on every training I focus on the different muscle groups for example :

Monday: Full Body (leg-focus)

Tuesday: Full Body (Chest-focus)

Wednesday: Full Body (Back focus)

Thursday: Rest

Friday : Rest + Kardio

Saturday: Full Body

Sunday : Full Body( leg-focus)

Full Body (leg-focus)

- In this training, I mainly focus on squats to improve my strength.

4x6 Squats

3x7 Pull-Ups + 3x10 Dumbell press 90s rest

3x5 Bulgarian squats + 3x12 Dumbell flies 90s rest

3x7 Hamstring extension + 3x6 Chin Up 90s rest

3x6 Hip thrust + 3x8 barbell rows 90s rest

Full Body (Chest-focus)

- This routine is focused on improving chest size.

(3x7) Pull-ups

(4x8) Dumbell press + (4x15) Calf raises

(3x10) Lunges + (3x12) Dips

(3x8) Dumbell flies + (3x5) Chin-ups

(3x7) Overhead Press + (3x12) Biceps curls

(3x7) Hip thrust + (to failure) Leg raises

Full Body (Back-focus)

- back is probably the most developed muscle on my body so my training for it is a little bit harder.

(3x7) Pull-ups + (3x12) Dumbell press 90s

(4x6) Chin-ups + (4x15) Calf raises 90s

(4x12) Bicep curls + (3x12) dumbell press 90s

(3x6) Sumo deadlift 90s
