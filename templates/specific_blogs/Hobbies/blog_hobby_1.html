Why I improve my focus

One of my hobbies is the satisfaction of being able to focus for a long period of time and get as much work as possible done in a short period of time.

One of the most influential things that I do to Improve my focus are : Meditation, Joggling, Reading, Working out.

Firstly I would like to start why I like to meditate. Meditation give me the power of being present and be able to focus only on my breath and nothing else for as long as I am capable of doing it ( no longer than 3 minutes now) also the meditation is good for my mental health, and I can be more present in social situations.

The second thing is Joggling is very similar to coding you need to maximize your focus for long as possible, so it gives you the ability to focus only on one thing at a time and nothing else.

Reading become really influential in my life when I discover the books Deep Work or Atomic Habits these books changed my life forever because I broke almost every bad habit that I have and changed it into the new ones that improved my life so much for example meditation or journaling.

Working out is my go-to when I am tired or my mind becomes a little bit cloudy or I get brain fog. When I am in the gym I am trying to focus on that one muscle that I am training. When I am trying to do something like this I try to feel every muscle fiber in that specific muscle group.
