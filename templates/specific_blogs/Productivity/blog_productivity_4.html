Flow state

I am a student so for me the flow state is really important because it keeps me focus and help me to do more work done. I am trying to get as much as 3-4 hours of uninterrupted work done in the morning. But not everyday is optimal so usually i get 3 hours or less but I am tryining to do it properly so this is my routine to get into the flow and get laser focused.

Flow ritual:

1. I clean my desk to not get any other thoughts in my head. (30s)
2. Drink some and get some water. (30s)
3. Get rid of my phone and turn notifications off and get rid of all distractions. (30s)
4. do the breathe-in breathe-out exercise to focus on “one thing in my head” (1 min)
5. Create a todo-list for the specific task that I am going to do and specify the things that need to be done and set it into order. (2-3min)
6. Start Working (90-180 min)
7. Long break (20-30 min)

This flow ritual I usually use for coding when I am learning or reading I only set up Pomodoro timer and don’t do any other preparation maybe the list of the things that needs to be done and then execute.

How to train yourself to do the flow state.

one of the greatest practices that I did at the beginning of my programming journey was using the Pomodoro timer for 10 min to work and 2 min rest and after some time with consistency, I was able to train myself to do uninterrupted work for at least one and a half hour to 3 hours and sometimes even more. But this comes with a practice and discipline like when you working out you don't see results after 2-3 days you start seeing something after 1-2 months.

The next good practice is to do mindfulness meditation or the wim-hof technique the right way for training your focus is to focus just on your breath or on the one point that you have in on your wall or something like that so just don't think “other ways” and focus on the one thing and keep your focus on the one point in your mind.

Reading is one the best things that people can do to improve their focus because you need to focus on the one core related subject and that is reading, thinking, and implementing the stuff that you learn into your brain and thinking about the time where you can use it and how you will use it in the future also you can take some notes. This type of activity where you learn, apply and take notes take all your focus.
