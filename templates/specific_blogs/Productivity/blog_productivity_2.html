Day structure

I structure my day based on the **80/20** rule 80% of outcome come from the 20 % of your work so you need to focus on things that will affect your life the most. I use the productivity hacked called einshowers matrix where i put the most important tasks to the top left and the less important to the right top.
Also I divide my work to shallow work and deep work ( I like to do the shallow work when I am in school this are for example email, reading about something, structuring learning path, etc...) when I am doing deep work i usually do the stuff that i am not able to do in school this is for example coding something or fixing bug learning and implementing etc..)
I also “filter” the tasks with with Morning, evening, afterSchool, Shallow Work, Deep Work and ASAP ( when I need to do something important)
