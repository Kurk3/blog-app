Daily routine

In this article you will learn how I structure my day to stay productive as long as possible and do the most work than in the shortest period of time also this article will contain only useful information that you can implement into your daily schedule if you find something usefull

Morning routine

When I woke up I drink a 1 l of water (to boost myself and get up faster)

Do some type of cardio to for 1-2 minutes (to stir up the blood flow)

After that, I do meditation for 2 minutes (to get myself present and focus on the stuff that I am doing I do the type of meditation where you focus only on your breath )

After that, I read my “rules” that consist of the how should I think and how to be in the positive mindset

Book or Youtube ( I watch videos about stoicism and code related stuff)

Breakfast ( oatmeal with blueberries or egg with toast and bread)

Summary :

I like to start a day being productive because it will set me up for the day I think that winning the morning is crucial.

Post School routine:

after I came home from school I go usually straight to the gym or get myself some exercise.

Exercise 45 min - 1 hour

Food

Coding (4-6 hours) I enter a **deep work** state and I use the Pomodoro technique to get myself tracked. (I use 60 to 10 frequency )

Reading books or Learning some interesting technologies theoretically. ( I don't code )

Summary :

This is the most important part of my day because I am coding or learning something that I am really interested in. I like to time-block everything from workouts to deep work sessions.

Evening routine

Journal ( I write my thoughts down and write the things that I’ve learned etc ... )

Write down the bad habits ( I usually write down the things that I’ve down badly that day. for example that I was on phone or that I wasn't focused enough.

Stretching ( I get myself a short stretching to get muscles relaxed and be relaxed also)

Sleep & Movie ( I like to fall asleep when with movies I sleep usually 7-8 hours to get myself rested for the next day)

Summary :

I strongly believe that if you can be 100% honest with yourself you can achieve everything you want in life but you need to trust the process and not focus on the outcome that you want to achieve because the outcome is the thing that you cannot change if you fail you need to know why you fail and improve your system rather than changing your goal.
