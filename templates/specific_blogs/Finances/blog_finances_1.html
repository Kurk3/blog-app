0 - 1 Principle

What is the 0 - 1 principle?

The simplest way to describe it is when you don't have something for example you don't have phone you are on 0 and when you have a phone you are in 1. So when you want to buy something for example you have iPhone 11 and you want to buy iPhone 13 how much it will improve your life and how the new phone will move you on scale in the 1.2 or something like that and that's when you might have to rethink your decision. “Is this really worth it ?” am I going to spend 1k dollars for this or should I have to invest it.

How I applied the 0-1 principle at my life ?

When I am thinking about buying something. I always apply this principle when I was buying new phone I was thinking about buying iPhone 10 or Xiaomi Note 10 pro difference was 300$

Firstly I ask myself. How can the phone improve my life what can I do with it and how does it help me to achieve my goal. When I asked this I absolutely threw the possibility of buying new iphone away and I bought the Xiaomi. and I made a good decision.

When I was buying my first car I asked myself what I wanted from the car. Do I need to look good or save money on gas? When I asked this me and my dad started watching cars that have low gas consumption and choose Renault Clio 3 2011 that have average consumption of 4.5 l for 100km and sometimes even lower. We are happy with that car and my father always say that it is the most comfortable car that he ever drive.

So when you want to apply the 0-1 principle ask yourself these questions.

What is my goal for that what i will do with it.

How often I often will i use it

Will it be 0- 1 or 1-1.3 or ?

Will the thing that I want to buy make me happy?

How much it will increase my productivity ?

In which areas will it help me.
