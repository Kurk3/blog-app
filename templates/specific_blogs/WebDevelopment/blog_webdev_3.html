My Frontend mistakes

My first mistake was that I don’t learn my programming editor properly I didn’t use shortcuts that will make my workflow much faster.

I didn’t understand web tools properly. When you understand web tools properly you can fix your CSS and javascript much faster on a lot of occasions.

Understand the difference between browsers before you implement the not so common feature in your code.

Find the plugins that fit your workflow and increase your development productivity dramatically.

Learn CSS properly and understand every basic property and its combination to avoid mistakes.

Take care of your health don’t waste time when you can’t solve the problem for longer than 4 hours. Take a short break.

Let others help you don’t waste time on everything that you can’t figure out.
